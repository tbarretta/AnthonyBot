What is Gift Registry?

Gift Registry is a private, invite-only app that lets family members share and manage their wishlists. Family members can view each other's wishlists, coordinate on gift purchases, and leave private comments — all without spoiling surprises for the wishlist owner.

How do I get access?

Gift Registry is invitation-only. A Family Admin must invite you by email. Once invited, you'll receive an email with a registration link.

  • Click the link in your invitation email to register
  • If the link has expired, click "Send Me a New Link" on the expiry page
  • If you already have an account, clicking an invitation link will add you to the new family automatically

Forgot your password?

Use the Forgot password? link on the sign-in page. A reset link will be sent to your email address.

Your Wishlist

  • Add items — Go to My Wishlist and click + Add Item. Each item has a name, description, price, desire rating (1–5 ⭐), an optional purchase link, and an optional photo.
  • Family visibility — When adding or editing an item, choose which families can see it. By default, all your families can see it.
  • Edit or delete — Click any item on your wishlist to edit it, or use the delete option to permanently remove it.
  • No longer wanted — Use Soft Remove to mark an item as no longer needed without deleting it. Others will no longer see it; you can undo this later.
  • Item limit — You can have up to 50 items on your wishlist at a time.

Viewing Other Members' Wishlists

  • Request access — Go to a Family page and click Request Access next to a member's name. They'll receive an email to approve or decline.
  • Once approved — A View Wishlist button appears. You can browse their items, mark items as purchased, and leave private comments.
  • Comments are hidden — Comments you leave on someone's item are never visible to that person — only to other family members who have access.
  • Mark as purchased — Marking an item as purchased hides it from others to prevent duplicate gifts. The item owner never sees who purchased it.
  • Declined access — If someone declines your request, you won't be able to re-request unless a Master Admin resets it.

Responding to Access Requests

  • When someone requests access to your wishlist, you'll receive an email with Approve and Decline links — no login required.
  • You can also approve or decline from the Dashboard or the Family page under Pending Access Requests.

Managed Members

  • What they are — Managed Members are profiles for children or elderly relatives who don't manage their own account. A guardian (you) manages their wishlist on their behalf.
  • Creating one — From your Dashboard, click + Add Managed Member. Enter their name and relationship (Child, Elder, or Other). They'll automatically join all your families.
  • Managing their wishlist — Click Manage Wishlist next to their name on your Dashboard. An amber banner will appear while you're managing their items.
  • Access requests — When someone requests access to a managed member's wishlist, you'll receive the notification and can approve or decline on their behalf.
  • Exiting — Click Exit ✕ in the amber banner to return to your own account.

Notifications & Preferences

  • Go to Preferences (click your name in the nav) to manage notification settings.
  • Access request emails — Toggle whether you receive an email when someone requests access to your wishlist.
  • New item alerts — Subscribe to receive an email whenever a specific family member adds something new to their wishlist.
  • Change password — Available at the bottom of the Preferences page. Requires your current password.

Family Admin

Inviting Members

  • From the Family Admin page (⚙️ Manage Family), enter a member's email under Invite Someone and click Send Invitation.
  • The invited person receives an email with a registration link valid for 7 days.
  • If they already have an account, they're added to the family immediately when they click the link.

Re-sending Expired Invitations

  • On the Family Admin page, pending invitations are listed under Pending Invitations.
  • Invitations that have expired show a red Expired badge. Click Resend to generate a new link and email it.
  • Members can also self-serve a new link by clicking Send Me a New Link when they visit an expired invitation URL.

Removing Members

  • On the Family Admin page under Members, click Remove next to any member (you cannot remove yourself).
  • Removing a member revokes their family access. Their wishlist items and account are not deleted.

Transferring Admin Rights

  • Click Make Admin next to a member's name on either the Family page or Family Admin page.
  • You'll see a warning explaining that your Admin rights will be removed once the other person accepts.
  • The selected member receives an email to Accept or Decline the transfer. No change takes effect until they accept.
  • Only one pending transfer is allowed at a time per family. Starting a new transfer cancels any previous pending one.

Family Theme

On the Family Admin page, choose from 6 themes under Family Theme. The theme applies to all members when they view this family's pages.

⚡ Master Admin

Master Admin Panel

Access the Master Admin panel via the ⚡ Admin link in the nav. From here you can see system-wide stats, search users, view the activity log, manage families, and reset passwords.

Creating Families

  • Use the Create Family panel on the Master Admin page.
  • Enter a family name and a Family Admin email. If the email already has an account, they're added as Admin immediately. Otherwise, an invitation is sent.

Managing Any Family

  • Click ⚡ Admin next to any family in the Families list to open the Master Admin family management page.
  • An amber banner confirms you are using elevated rights. From here you can Make Admin or Remove any member, and resend pending invitations.
  • Make Admin follows the same email acceptance flow as Family Admin transfers — the selected member must accept via email before the change takes effect.

Resetting Passwords

On the Master Admin page, find the user in the Users list and click Reset PW. A password reset email will be sent to them. This is logged in the Activity Log.

Activity Log

The Activity Log on the Master Admin page records all significant actions system-wide — access requests, approvals, family changes, password resets, and Master Admin overrides. Use the event type filter to narrow results.

Deleting Families

Click Delete next to a family on the Master Admin page. You'll see a confirmation page listing any members who will lose their only family. Deleting a family removes all memberships, invitations, and access requests for that family. Individual user accounts and wishlist items are not deleted.
